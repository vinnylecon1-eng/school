Academics

We offer a wide range of subjects at both Ordinary Level (O-Level) and Advanced Level (A-Level). Our curriculum includes sciences, arts, and business studies, taught by experienced educators committed to excellence.

  • O-Level: Mathematics, English, Biology, Physics, Chemistry, History, Geography, Kiswahili, Civics, French language
